SenseVoice

A speech AI project in my working stack for multilingual transcription, language recognition, emotion cues, and fast audio understanding experiments.

Core Use

Speech Understanding

SenseVoice supports ASR, language identification, emotion recognition, and acoustic event detection in one model family.

Why It Matters

Fast Prototype Layer

It is useful when I need a lightweight speech pipeline for research notes, transcripts, multilingual audio review, or quick testing.
